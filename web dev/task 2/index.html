Srushti Jayant Deshmukh

Srushti Jayant Deshmukh is an Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer who secured the All India Rank 5 in the UPSC Civil Services Examination in 2018. She hails from Kasturba Nagar in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, and completed her schooling from the local Carmel Convent School. She graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering from Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya in Bhopal. After completing her engineering degree, Srushti worked as a Business Analyst with the Royal Bank of Scotland for two years. However, her desire to serve the country and make a positive impact on society led her to pursue the civil services. As an IAS officer, Srushti has been serving in the Maharashtra cadre since 2020. She has been appointed as the Assistant Collector and Assistant Magistrate of Pandharpur in Solapur district of Maharashtra. She has also been involved in various initiatives aimed at improving the lives of people in the district, such as promoting digital literacy and launching a water conservation campaign. Srushti is an inspiration to many young aspirants who wish to pursue a career in civil services and serve the country
